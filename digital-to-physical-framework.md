## Executive Summary

### **The Challenge:**
Physical events are high-value but low-reach. Digital content is high-reach but low-depth. How do we combine them?

### **The Solution:**
Use digital channels as **acquisition funnels** for physical events, then use physical events to create **digital content** that fuels the next cycle.

### **The Flywheel:**
```
DIGITAL CONTENT (blog, social, email)
    ‚Üì
AWARENESS (10,000 reach)
    ‚Üì
EVENT PROMOTION (targeted to segment)
    ‚Üì
PHYSICAL ATTENDANCE (100 qualified attendees)
    ‚Üì
DEEP RELATIONSHIPS (1-on-1 conversations)
    ‚Üì
CONTENT CREATION (event recap, testimonials)
    ‚Üì
DIGITAL DISTRIBUTION (10,000+ reach)
    ‚Üì
REPEAT (next event bigger, better qualified)
```

### **Success Metrics:**
- **Digital ‚Üí Physical Conversion:** 5-10% of digital audience attends physical events
- **Physical ‚Üí Digital Amplification:** 1 physical attendee = 10 digital shares
- **Network Effect:** Each cycle grows digital audience by 20-30%
