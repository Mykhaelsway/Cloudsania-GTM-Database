# Phase 0 & Phase 1 Execution Roadmap

**Project:** Cloudsania GTM Strategy - Flywheel Launch  
**Timeline:** 16 weeks (Q1 ending/Q2 2026)  
**Team Size:** 5-10 members  
**Budget:** Bootstrapped  
**Primary Market:** Lagos, Nigeria (expanding to Nairobi, Accra)

---

## Executive Summary

**Phase 0 (Weeks 1-4): Foundation**
- Website revamp (5 landing pages + Community + Partners pages)
- Social media profile optimization
- Pre-sensitization campaign ("We've Evolved")

**Phase 1 (Weeks 5-16): Seeding the Flywheel**
- Agency Partnership Program (47% segment priority)
- Build in Public Program (33% segment priority)
- Office Hours + Physical Hub Activations (Lagos)
- SEO Content Factory
- WhatsApp Communities
- High-Value Downloadable Assets

**Success Metrics:**
- **Phase 0:** 500+ website visitors, 50+ email opens, 20+ social engagements
- **Phase 1:** 100+ signups, 5+ agency partners, 30+ case studies/testimonials, 1,000+ organic visitors/month

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Low agency interest** | Medium | High | Start with warm intros, offer pilot discounts |
| **Build in Public low applications** | Medium | Medium | Hybrid model: 70% application, 30% invite |
| **Office Hours low attendance** | Medium | Medium | Partner with established hubs, promote 2 weeks in advance |
| **Content production delays** | High | Medium | Use freelancers, create content buffer |
| **Website launch bugs** | Medium | High | 2-week QA period, soft launch to existing users first |
| **Budget overruns** | Low | Medium | Prioritize free/low-cost tactics, cut paid tools if needed |
