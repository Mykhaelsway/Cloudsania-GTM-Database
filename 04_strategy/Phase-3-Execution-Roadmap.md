## Risk Mitigation

<!-- TABLE: Phase 3 Risks (Columns: Risk, Probability, Impact, Mitigation) -->

**Hackathon low ROI:**
- Probability: Medium
- Impact: High
- Mitigation: Focus on developer-heavy hackathons, offer compelling prizes, hands-on workshop

**Automation complexity:**
- Probability: High
- Impact: Medium
- Mitigation: Start with simple sequences, iterate based on data, use proven email tools

**Demo Shop low walk-ins:**
- Probability: Medium
- Impact: Medium
- Mitigation: Promote 1 week in advance, partner with hub for cross-promotion

**University partnership delays:**
- Probability: Medium
- Impact: Low
- Mitigation: Start outreach in Week 37, have backup universities ready

**Content production burnout:**
- Probability: High
- Impact: Medium
- Mitigation: Hire additional freelancers, build 3-week content buffer
