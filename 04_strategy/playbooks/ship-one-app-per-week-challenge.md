# Ship One App Per Week Challenge

**Program Type:** Advanced Build in Public Track  
**Duration:** 4 weeks  
**Target:** AI Natives, Agencies, Indie Builders  
**Timeline:** Launch Week 9 (Phase 1)

---

## Challenge Structure

**Week 1:** Deploy MVP  
**Week 2:** Add feature + ship update  
**Week 3:** Add another feature + ship update  
**Week 4:** Polish + ship final version

**Incentive:** Complete all 4 weeks → Free Pro tier for 6 months

---

## Eligibility

- Active in Build in Public program
- Completed at least 1 deployment on Cloudsania
- Active on Twitter/LinkedIn (for public updates)

---

## Requirements

**Weekly:**
- Deploy 1 app or major update
- Share build log (Twitter/LinkedIn)
- Document deployment process

**Final Deliverable:**
- 4 deployed apps OR 1 app with 4 major updates
- 4 public build logs
- 1 case study (300-500 words)

---

## Rewards

**Completion (all 4 weeks):**
- Free Pro tier for 6 months ($474 value)
- Featured on Cloudsania homepage
- Case study published on blog
- Priority support

**Partial Completion (2-3 weeks):**
- Free Starter tier for 3 months
- Community spotlight

---

## Content Output

**Per Builder:** 4 case studies (1 per week)  
**Per Cohort:** 40 case studies (10 builders × 4 weeks)

---

## Success Metrics

- 10 builders complete challenge
- 40 case studies published
- 50% conversion to paid after 6-month free tier
