## 1. Executive Summary: The "Security Feature" Sell
**Key Finding:** Salus Cloud positions itself heavily on **Functional Security** (Scanning/Remediation) rather than **Outcome Compliance** (SOC2/GDPR).

*   **Primary Hook:** "Vulnerability Management" & "Automated Remediation".
*   **Missing Hook:** "Audit Readiness" or "Compliance Frameworks" (GDPR) are surprisingly absent from top-level messaging.
*   **Identity Check:** "Salus Security" is confirmed as a separate Web3 entity (excluded).

**Strategic Implication:**
Salus operates in the "DevSecOps" niche, selling to technical leads who worry about *vulnerabilities*. They are NOT yet selling effectively to CEO/Founders who worry about *fines/compliance*. This is a major gap.
