# Lagos Physical Tech Hubs & Coworking Inventory

**Goal:** Identify ~20 tech-forward spaces for physical activation, partnerships, and agency outreach.

| Hub Name | Location | "Tech Vibe" | Notable For | Website/Contact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CcHUB** | Yaba (Herbert Macaulay) | **Dev Heavy** | The OG hub. Mark Zuckerberg visited here. Global acclaim. | [cchub.africa](https://cchub.africa) |
| **Leadspace** | Yaba (Ojuelegba) | **Startup/Creative** | High density of "Vibe Coders" and early founders. | [theleadspace.co](https://theleadspace.co) |
| **CivicHive** | Yaba (Montgomery Rd) | **Social Impact/Gov** | BudgIT HQ. Great for contacting GovTech agencies. | [civichive.org](https://civichive.org) |

| **Bridge by Obsidian** | Akoka (Univ Rd) | **Creative/Student** | Adjacent to Unilag. Student pipeline central. | [bridgebyobsidian.com](https://bridgebyobsidian.com) |
