## 5. Summary of Critical Intervention Points

1.  **Deployment Simplicity Proof**: All segments require proof that "Git push = deploy" works without DevOps knowledge. (Critical at Decision stage)
2.  **Agency Dashboard Demo**: Agencies need to see "50 sites, one dashboard" in action. (Critical at Consideration stage)
3.  **AI-Native Deployment Simplicity**: Builders need proof that "it works on localhost" means "it works in production"—without learning DevOps. (Critical at Consideration stage: AI-01, AI-04, AI-07)
4.  **Traditional Dev DevOps Fear**: Traditional devs need reassurance that no Docker/Kubernetes knowledge is required. (Critical at Consideration stage)
5.  **Startup Compliance Proof**: Funded startups require SOC2/ISO validation before considering simplicity. (Critical at Consideration stage)

## 6. Phygital Journey Paths

### Path A: Physical-First Journey
**Entry Point:** Physical event (workshop, conference, meetup)
**Bridge:** QR code scan + 2-question quiz

```
PHYSICAL EVENT (Workshop/Conference/Meetup)
        ↓
    Meet team, see demo, experience product
        ↓
QR SCAN (Business card or screen)
        ↓
SEGMENTATION QUIZ (2 questions)
    Q1: "What's your role?" → Determines segment
    Q2: "What's your biggest challenge?" → Determines pain point
        ↓
PERSONALIZED LANDING PAGE (Segment-specific)
        ↓
TRIAL START (Event-contextualized)
        ↓
ONBOARDING SEQUENCE (Event variant, Light intensity)
        ↓
CUSTOMER
        ↓
ADVOCATE (Returns to events, brings new leads)
```

**Key Characteristics:**
- Highest intent (attended event specifically)
- Light nurture needed (already convinced)
- Fast conversion (7-14 days)
- Direct CTA style ("Start now")

---

### Path B: Digital-First with Physical Acceleration
**Entry Point:** Digital discovery (blog, social, ad)
**Acceleration:** Geo-targeted event invitation

```
DIGITAL AWARENESS (Blog, Social, Ad, Search)
        ↓
DIGITAL CONSIDERATION (Email nurture, content)
        ↓
GEO-TARGETED EVENT INVITATION
    "You're in Lagos - join us at [Event]"
        ↓
PHYSICAL EVENT ATTENDANCE
        ↓
QR SCAN → Trial (or deepened relationship)
        ↓
CUSTOMER
```

**Key Characteristics:**
- Medium intent (discovered digitally, chose to attend)
- Physical event accelerates decision
- Converts leads stuck in consideration stage
- Hybrid nurture (started digital, finished physical)

---

### Path C: Virtual-to-Physical (Hybrid)
**Entry Point:** Virtual event (webinar, online workshop)
**Conversion:** City-based physical event invitation

```
VIRTUAL EVENT ATTENDANCE (Webinar, online workshop)
        ↓
POST-EVENT SEGMENTATION
    Capture city/location from registration
        ↓
CITY-SPECIFIC PHYSICAL INVITATION
    "You attended our webinar - now meet us in Lagos"
        ↓
PHYSICAL EVENT ATTENDANCE
        ↓
QR SCAN → Deep conversion
        ↓
CUSTOMER
```

**Key Characteristics:**
- Virtual qualifies interest
- Physical deepens relationship
- Tests local demand before hosting
- Extends reach beyond physical geography

## 8. Customer-to-Advocate Flywheel Loop

Customers don't just convert and stay - they become advocates who fuel the next generation of leads.

### The Loop

```
CUSTOMER (Active user, happy with product)
        ↓
EVENT INVITATION (Exclusive for customers)
    "Share your experience at our next workshop"
        ↓
EVENT ATTENDANCE (As speaker/mentor/participant)
    - Speaks about their success
    - Mentors new attendees
    - Provides social proof
        ↓
CONTENT CREATION (Testimonial, case study, UGC)
    - Video testimonial (captured at event)
    - Case study (written post-event)
    - Social posts (tagged, shared)
        ↓
REFERRAL (Brings colleagues/peers to next event)
    "My colleague should see this"
        ↓
NEW LEADS ENTER FUNNEL
        ↓
FLYWHEEL COMPOUNDS (Bigger audience, more social proof)
```

### Customer Event Participation Types

| Participation | Effort | Value | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Attendee** | Low | Medium | Shows up, talks to new leads |
| **Testimonial Giver** | Low | High | 30-60 sec video captured at event |
| **Panelist** | Medium | High | 15-min panel discussion |
| **Demo Presenter** | Medium | Very High | Shows their app as case study |
| **Workshop Mentor** | High | Very High | Helps new users hands-on |
| **Referral Source** | Low | Very High | Brings colleagues/peers |

### Customer Event Incentives

| Action | Incentive |
|:---|:---|
| Attend event | Priority seating, exclusive swag |
| Give testimonial | Featured on website case study page |
| Present on panel | Co-marketing distribution to 10k list |
| Demo their app | "Builder of the Month" YouTube feature |
| Mentor new users | "Champion" status, partner perks |
| Bring referrals | Exclusive partner merchandise |
