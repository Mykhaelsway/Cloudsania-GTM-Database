# Salus Cloud - Initial Competitor Profile
**Date:** 2026-01-26
**Status:** Phase 1 Discovery

## 1. Top-Level Overview
- **Founded:** 2024 by Andrew Mori (ex-CTO at Deimos).
- **Funding:** $3.7M Seed (Atlantica Ventures involved).
- **Geo Focus:** Africa & Middle East, but product is "AI-Native".
- **Core Value Prop:** "AI-Native DevSecOps." Merges deployment with security/compliance.

## 2. Product Capabilities (Initial Scan)
- **Deployment:** Zero-touch deployments, intelligent configuration.
- **Security:** Built-in vulnerability scanning, secret management, compliance audit records.
- **AI-Ops:** Automated cost optimization, log analysis, anomaly detection.
- **Infrastructure:** Multi-cloud/Hybrid support.
- **Modules:** "Salus3" adds asset tracking, HR, CRM modules (suggests broader ERP-like play? needs verification).

## 3. Pricing Model
- **Free Plan:** 1 seat, unlimited projects, 7-day logs.
- **Developer:** $29/user/mo. Unlimited seats, 120 build mins.
- **Team:** $99/mo (up to 5 users). ~$19.80/user effective.
- **Business:** $299/mo.
- **Enterprise:** Custom (AWS Marketplace listing $25k/yr).

## 4. Market Positioning
- **Vs Standard PaaS:** Positions as "DevSecOps" platform, not just hosting.
- **Vs PipeOps:** Higher end, more focus on security/compliance and AI. Higher funding ($3.7M vs $20k).
- **Target Audience:** Startups/SMEs in emerging markets needing compliance/security out of the box.

## 5. Reviews and Sentiment
- **Sentiment:** Strong on specific tech blogs (Deimos connection).
- **Key Claims:** "Deploy in under 5 minutes," "Zero-touch."
