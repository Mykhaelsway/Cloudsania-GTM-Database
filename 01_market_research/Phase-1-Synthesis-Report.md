# Phase 1 Synthesis Report: Competitor Discovery
**Date:** 2026-01-26
**Status:** Phase 1 Complete

## Executive Summary
Phase 1 discovery confirms the initial hypothesis: The African "Modern PaaS" market is a massive **White Space**.
- **Direct Competitors:** Only **2** credible "modern" platforms exist (PipeOps, Salus Cloud).
- **The Rest:** 90% of the market is dominated by "Legacy IaaS" (VPS/cPanel providers) or "Global Giants" (Heroku/Vercel).
- **Opportunity:** There is no dominant "Vercel for Africa" yet. PipeOps is the low-end entry, Salus Cloud is the high-end enterprise/security play. Cloudsania has a clear lane in the middle (Professional/Agency).
